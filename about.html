About Us


The House of India welcomes you to the world of exotic and exciting Indian Cuisine in Concord.

We have put together a menu that shall please your palate and exercise your imagination.



Traditional recipes have been followed with some changes to give you a healthy and varied menu.

The food is not "spicy" however, spiciness can be varied to suit your taste.

So sit back, relax, and enjoy the traditional Indian hospitality.
